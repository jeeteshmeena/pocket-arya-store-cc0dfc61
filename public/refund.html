Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

⚡ Quick Summary: We offer a full refund within 24 hours of purchase if content has not been accessed. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days via the original payment method.

1. Digital Product Nature

AryaPremium Store sells digital subscriptions and online content. Once digital content is accessed or downloaded, it cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense. Our refund policy is designed to be fair while accounting for the nature of digital goods.

2. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 24 hours of purchase.
  • The digital content has not been accessed or downloaded.
  • Technical issues on our end prevented access to purchased content.
  • You were charged incorrectly or charged multiple times for the same item.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Content has been accessed, streamed, or downloaded.
  • Refund request is made after 24 hours of purchase.
  • Change of mind after accessing the product.
  • Incompatibility issues that were clearly stated in product description.
  • Violation of our Terms of Service leading to account suspension.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact our support team within 24 hours of purchase:

  • Email: support@aryapremium.store
  • Include your Order ID, registered email/Telegram ID, and reason for refund.

Our team will respond within 24 hours of receiving your request.

5. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5–7 business days to appear on your statement.
  • UPI: 2–3 business days.
  • Net Banking: 3–5 business days.
  • Wallets: 1–3 business days.

Refunds are processed via Razorpay to the original payment method used during purchase.

6. Cancellation Policy

Since AryaPremium Store offers one-time purchase digital products (not recurring subscriptions), there is no automatic cancellation needed. Each purchase grants lifetime access to the specific digital content purchased.

7. Partial Refunds

In cases where a bundle was purchased and only part of the content is disputed, we may offer a partial refund at our discretion based on the value of the specific item(s) in question.

8. Contact for Disputes

For any disputes or escalations, contact us at: support@aryapremium.store

We aim to resolve all disputes within 48 hours of receipt.
