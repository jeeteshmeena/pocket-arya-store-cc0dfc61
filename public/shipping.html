Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

⚡ Digital Delivery Only: AryaPremium Store sells exclusively digital products. There is no physical shipping. All purchases are delivered electronically within seconds of payment confirmation.

1. Nature of Products

All products and subscriptions available on AryaPremium Store (aryapremium.store) are 100% digital in nature. We do not sell, ship, or deliver any physical goods.

2. Delivery Method

Digital content is delivered through the following methods:

  • Instant Electronic Access: Content becomes accessible immediately upon payment confirmation.
  • Telegram Delivery: For Telegram-based subscriptions, access is granted to your Telegram account linked at checkout.
  • Platform Access: Direct access via our secure digital platform at aryapremium.store/aryaPremium.

3. Delivery Timeframe

  • Standard Digital Delivery: Immediate (within seconds of payment confirmation).
  • In case of technical delay: Maximum 24 hours. If your access is not granted within 24 hours, please contact our support.

4. Delivery Issues

If you have not received access to your purchased content within 24 hours of payment, please contact us with:

  • Your Order ID or Transaction ID.
  • Your registered Telegram username or email.
  • A screenshot of the payment confirmation.

Contact: support@aryapremium.store

5. International Customers

Since our products are digital, they are available to customers worldwide. However, pricing is in Indian Rupees (INR) and payments are processed through Razorpay, which supports international cards.

6. No Physical Shipping

AryaPremium Store does not ship any physical products. If you received any communication regarding physical shipment, please treat it as fraudulent and report it to us immediately.
