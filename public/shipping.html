Delivery Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Important: AryaPremium Store sells exclusively digital products. All purchases are delivered electronically. No physical goods are shipped under any circumstances.

1. Nature of Products

All products and subscriptions listed on AryaPremium Store (aryapremium.store) are 100% digital in nature. We do not manufacture, stock, or ship any physical goods. By completing a purchase, the customer acknowledges that they are buying a digital service or digital content subscription.

2. Delivery Methods

Digital content and subscriptions are delivered through the following electronic methods:

  • Platform Access: Purchased content becomes immediately accessible through our secure digital platform upon payment confirmation.
  • Account-Based Delivery: Access is granted to the customer's registered account or digital identity linked at the time of checkout.
  • Email Confirmation: An order confirmation and access details are sent to the customer's registered email address upon successful payment.

3. Delivery Timeframes

  • Standard Delivery: Immediate — access is granted within seconds of payment confirmation under normal circumstances.
  • In the event of a technical delay: A maximum of 24 hours from the time of successful payment. If access is not granted within this window, the customer should contact our support team.

4. Delivery Confirmation

Successful delivery is defined as the point at which access to purchased digital content is made available to the customer through our platform. An email confirmation is sent to the registered address as acknowledgment of the completed transaction.

5. Failed or Delayed Delivery

If you have not received access to your purchased digital content within 24 hours of payment, please contact us with the following details:

  • Order ID or Payment Transaction ID
  • Registered email address used at checkout
  • A screenshot or copy of the payment confirmation receipt

Contact: support@aryapremium.store

We will investigate and resolve all delivery issues within 24 hours of receiving your report.

6. No Physical Shipping

AryaPremium Store does not ship any physical product to any location within India or internationally. If any communication you have received implies physical shipping from AryaPremium Store, please treat it as unauthorized and report it to us immediately at support@aryapremium.store.

7. Geographic Availability

Our digital products are available to customers across India. International access may also be possible, subject to applicable payment processing availability. Since all delivery is electronic, geographic location does not affect delivery timelines.

8. Contact

For delivery-related queries: support@aryapremium.store or visit our Contact Page.
